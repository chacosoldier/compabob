# Code of Conduct

This is a small, single-maintainer project. The policy is short on purpose.

## The standard

Be decent. Assume good faith. Critique ideas, not people.

In practice that means:

- **Welcome.** Beginners, non-native English speakers, and people new to the terminal are explicitly welcome. The kit ships a no-terminal-experience walkthrough for a reason.
- **Not welcome.** Harassment, personal attacks, demeaning comments, or sustained disruption in any project space (issues, pull requests, discussions).

This applies to everyone taking part: contributors, the maintainer, and anyone commenting.

## If something goes wrong

Report it privately to the maintainer at **phiwenger@gmail.com**.

The maintainer will look into it and may edit, lock, or delete content, or block an account, depending on what happened. Decisions are final and at the maintainer's discretion.

## Attribution

This is a lightweight policy suited to a project of this size. For larger communities, the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org) is the recognized standard and worth adopting instead.
